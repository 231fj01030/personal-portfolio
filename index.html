About bose

ose was a key figure in the Indian National Congress and initially worked under leaders like Mahatma Gandhi. However, he soon grew disillusioned with their non-violent methods and became a strong advocate for more aggressive tactics against British rule. His leadership and ideas led to a division within the Congress, and he formed the Forward Bloc in 1939, a political group focused on pushing for more direct resistance to the British.
